About Me

Hi! My name is Victoria Day, and I'm a sophomore at Brigham Young University originally from Chadds Ford, Pennsylvania—just 30 minutes from Philadelphia. I'm currently a Pre-Business major and plan to apply this spring to the Experience Design and Management program at the Marriott School of Business. I'm also pursuing double minors in Anthropology and through BYU's Honors Program.

I currently serve as a Teaching Assistant for BYU's Anthropology 101 course and bring over three years of experience in the food and restaurant industry. These experiences have strengthened my love for people, process, and productivity—whether I'm leading teams or serving behind the counter.

I'm passionate, hardworking, flexible, and communicative. I pride myself on being a team player and supportive friend, and I thrive in collaborative environments where I can grow as a leader and learner. My goal is to expand my technical and professional skills while exploring opportunities in the global business sphere.

Skills: Production Preparation, Multitasking, Communication, Organizational Mindset, Adaptability, Microsoft Office Suite, SQL Experience

Interests: Tennis, Jazz Piano, Hiking, Yoga, Poetry, Skiing, Event Planning, Social Impact & Justice, Volunteering
